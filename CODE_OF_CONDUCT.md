# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Open Typing Trainer to be useful for contributors from many countries, languages, schools, and communities. We pledge to make participation respectful, inclusive, and focused on constructive collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and professional language.
- Respect different languages, cultures, accessibility needs, and educational contexts.
- Give and receive technical feedback in good faith.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project.
- Credit contributors and sources where appropriate.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Sexualized content or unwanted attention.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions or maintainer work.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior conflicts with this Code of Conduct. Enforcement should be proportionate, consistent, and focused on protecting the project community.

## Reporting

If you see or experience unacceptable behavior, contact the project maintainers through the repository's public issue or discussion channels unless privacy requires a direct maintainer contact.

Reports should be handled with care and, where possible, confidentiality.
